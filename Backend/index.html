Project Proposal: Bajeti Tracker - A Personal Finance Management App

1. Title:

Bajeti Tracker - A Personal Finance Management App

2. Introduction:

The Bajeti Tracker app is designed to assist users in tracking their expenses, managing their budget and setting savings goals. In an era where financial literacy is increasingly important, this app aims to provide users with a convenient tool to monitor their spending habits and achieve their financial goals effectively.

3. Project Overview:

The Bajeti Tracker app will enable users to input their income, expenses, and saving goals. It will offer visual representations of their financial data, such as charts and graphs, to facilitate a better understanding of their financial status. Additionally, the app will provide personalized recommendations and tips to improve financial health.

4. Objectives:

  • Real-Time Expense Tracking: Enable users to track their expenses and income in real-time.
  • Spending Insights: Provide users with insights into their spending habits and areas for improvement.
  • Savings Goals: Allow users to set and track their savings goals.
  • Financial Advice: Offer personalized financial advice and tips for better money management.

5. Technical Requirements:

  • Front-End Development: Utilize HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the user interface.
  • Back-End Development: Employ a back-end framework like Node.js or Django for server-side processing.
  • Database: Use a database management system like MongoDB or PostgreSQL for data storage and retrieval.
